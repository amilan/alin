3.1. Control and Status

HW address: 0x0

C offset: 0x0

7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0
- SP1 READ_MODE[1:0] LED[2:0] RELAY
  • RELAY [read/write]: FV_relay_sta
    On reading the state of the FV relay. 1 - FV on.
    On write changes the state of FV relay if FV Voltage is in the correct range.
  • LED [read-only]: FV_Led_sta
    The state of FV_led 0 means Led is off.
  • READ_MODE [read/write]: Reading_mode
    The Reading mode of Floating voltage.
    1. Reading of instantaneus voltage.
    2. Reading of minimum voltage since last reading.
    3. Reading of minimum voltage since last reading.
    4. Reading of average voltage of 256 samples.
  • SP1 [read/write]: Spare Led
    Sets or gets the spare led. 1 -> Led on
  • FV [read-only]: Floating ground Voltage
    The floating voltage is: 2.5*(1001*FV/1024 - 500)

  • MAX [read/write]: Maximum Voltage
    The maximum voltage before switching off the FV_Relay signal
    The voltage follows the following formula V=2.5*(1001*Max/1024 - 500)
  • MIN [read/write]: Minimum voltage
    The minimum voltage before switching off the FV_Relay signal
    The voltage follows the following formula V=2.5*(1001*Min/1024 - 500)
